Meet Lauren

Who is Lauren?

Hi, I'm Lauren! I am a senior at University of Colorado at Boulder studying computer science and mathematics. I am President of CU Women in Computing, an ACM-W chapter. In my free time you will most likely find me running, skiing, coding, or shopping for anything pink.

CU Women in Computing

CU Women in Computing (CUWIC) is the ACM-W chapter within the Computer Science Department at the University of Colorado at Boulder that is dedicated to supporting women and diversity in computing. We encourage interaction from all who are interested in issues regarding women and diversity in computing to create a forum in which to share information about career, academic, and social opportunities.
